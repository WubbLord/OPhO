OPHO Home Page

The Online Physics Olympiad (OPhO) is an annual team based physics competition for both high school students and undergraduates of all levels. Originally inspired by Physics Brawl Online, the competition started in 2020 during COVID-19, and thousands of participants from around the world has participated since. Teams of up to three will compete in the open round and invitational round.

The 2023 open contest will take place from July 22-24 and the 2023 invitational contest will take place from August 5-6.

Registration has closed with 851 teams! Emails with further instructions will be sent to teams soon.
