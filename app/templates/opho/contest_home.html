OPHO Home Page

The Online Physics Olympiad (OPhO) is an annual team based physics competition for both high school students and undergraduates of all levels. Originally inspired by Physics Brawl Online, the competition started in 2020 during COVID-19, and thousands of participants from around the world has participated since. Teams of up to three will compete in the open round and invitational round. For all rules, click here.
